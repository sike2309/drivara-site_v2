Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting your first quote.

Is it really free?
Yes — 100% free for car owners, forever. You describe your problem, workshops compete for your job. No hidden charges, no commission taken from you.
How fast will I get quotes?
Most jobs receive the first quote within 30 minutes. Popular repairs like brake pads or oil changes often get 3–5 quotes within an hour.
Are the workshops verified?
Every workshop on Drivara is manually reviewed before listing. You can also see real ratings and reviews from previous customers before you decide.
What if I'm not satisfied?
You're never locked in. You choose which quote to accept — and you can chat directly with the workshop before committing to anything.
Which areas are covered?
We're currently active across Klang Valley (KL, PJ, Shah Alam, Subang, Cheras) and expanding to Johor Bahru and Penang in 2026.
Do I need to go to the workshop first?
No. Just describe the issue and upload a photo or short video. Workshops will diagnose and quote based on that — you only visit once you've accepted a quote.
